Coming Soon

The VVP Issuer service is under development. When complete, it will support:

  • Identity Management - Create and manage KERI identifiers with witness backing
  • Credential Registry - Issue and revoke ACDC credentials via TEL
  • vLEI Credentials - Legal Entity, Authorized Public Entity, and Designated Entity credentials
  • TN Allocation - Telephone number allocation credentials for caller authentication
  • Dossier Assembly - Bundle credentials into verifiable dossiers

Credential Types

LE Legal Entity

Root credential binding an LEI to a KERI identifier. Issued by GLEIF-authorized vLEI issuers.

APE Authorized Public Entity

Role authorization within an organization. Enables delegation of signing authority.

TN TN Allocation

Telephone number allocation credential. Proves rights to originate calls from specific numbers.

Architecture

The issuer service uses KERI primitives from keripy:

Habery
Identity management with key rotation support
Regery
Credential registry for issuance and revocation tracking
Witnesses
Distributed receipts for key event and TEL anchoring
